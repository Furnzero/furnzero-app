OUR STORY

A world with zero furniture waste

In a world where the furniture industry is essential for creating comfort in our spaces, the industry's impact on the environment is often overlooked. In the Netherlands alone, millions of kilograms of furniture are discarded annually, with only 10% recycled. The rest ends up in landfills, contributing to a growing environmental burden.

Our mission is towards zero furniture waste by helping furniture producers simplify end-of-life management to reduce landfill waste. We optimise end-to-end waste solutions and digital tools to ensure responsible supply chains with the goal of closing the loop on furniture waste. Our solution unfolds in two major phases with the initial focus on extending the lifespan of furniture products. We do this by connecting discarded furniture from consumers and businesses with recycling companies to ensure items are collected and processed in a circular manner. This phase directly addresses the challenges of disposal by making it accessible and hassle-free. Building on this foundation, FurnZero will also offer a one-stop data portal to increase supply chain transparency using AI and blockchain. This will engage supply chain stakeholders across the furniture industry to identify lifecycle inefficiencies, from material composition, waste generation, and end life management.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our services, processes, and how we contribute to reducing furniture waste sustainably.

1. How does your furniture collection service work?

Our collection service makes it easy for consumers and businesses to responsibly dispose of unwanted furniture and mattresses. Simply schedule a pickup through our platform, and we'll ensure the items are collected dry making it possible for them to be recycled with certified recyclers or given a second life with NGOs.

2. What types of furniture do you refurbish?

We refurbish a wide range of items, including mattresses, chairs, tables, and storage units. Our goal is to restore these items to high-quality standards for resale to budget-conscious and circular-minded consumers.

3. How do you ensure sustainability in your processes?

We partner with certified recyclers, furniture refurbishments to extend its lifecycle, and use data-driven tools like AI and blockchain to optimize supply chains and minimize waste.

4. Who can benefit from your services?

Our services cater to individuals, businesses, housing providers, and any organization seeking a convenient solution for furniture disposal. We also offer tools for tracking furniture lifecycles, reducing waste generation and enhance transparency within the production process in line with circular economy goals.

5. What does your data portal offer?

The data portal offers lifecycle analysis, supply chain transparency, insights on alternative circular materials, and compliance tools for manufacturers to meet EU regulations and embrace sustainability.
